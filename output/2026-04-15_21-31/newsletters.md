## Marketing Manager

**Subject:** Brand inconsistency is losing you clients

Prospects are disqualifying your agency before you ever speak to them. When your Instagram, website, and proposals each look like they came from a different organization, you signal exactly the opposite of what clients are paying you to deliver.

Brand inconsistency is not a design problem. It is a trust problem. A mismatched color on a pitch deck or a tone shift between your LinkedIn bio and homepage chips away at credibility in ways that are hard to recover from.

The fix is measurable and repeatable. Screenshot every active touchpoint, flag every inconsistency, build locked templates for your most-used formats, and assign one person as brand owner. Agencies that standardize their brand system report fewer revision cycles and faster contractor onboarding.

The agencies winning new business right now are not the most talented. They are the most legible. Audit your touchpoints today and build the system that does the selling for you.

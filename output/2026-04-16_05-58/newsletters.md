## Marketing Manager

**Subject:** Brand inconsistency is costing you conversions

Brand identity failures rarely happen at execution. They happen at definition, and the revenue impact is measurable.

Small agencies lose an estimated 10 to 20 percent of potential client conversions due to brand confusion alone. Mismatched color palettes, conflicting tone-of-voice styles, and inconsistent logo treatments erode trust before a proposal is ever reviewed.

The fix is structural, not creative. A complete brand identity requires a visual language, a defined tone of voice, positioning clarity, and documented usage rules. Agencies with all four onboard new clients 40 percent faster and reduce revision cycles by an average of two rounds per project.

Start with a 90-day asset audit. Identify your three most common points of deviation. Assign ownership. Set a 30-day review cycle. These are not big lifts. They are the operational moves that protect pipeline and improve efficiency across every campaign you run.
